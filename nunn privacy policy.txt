2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you use the Service:

- Contact Information: Email addresses or other contact details you provide when communicating with us.
- User Content: Questions, comments, or other materials you submit through the Service.

2.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain information when you interact with the Service:

- Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and timestamps. We anonymize or mask personally identifiable information (PII) where appropriate.
- Usage Patterns: Usage patterns and interaction metrics.
- Performance Analytics: Performance analytics, error logs, and debugging information. Error logs may contain query metadata needed for troubleshooting, but we strive to minimize the inclusion of PII.
- Cookies and Similar Technologies: Information collected through cookies to enhance user experience. We use essential cookies for session management and may use analytics cookies for improving the Service. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

2.3 Third-Party Data

Our Service integrates with third-party APIs and services:

- API Providers: We use APIs from OpenRouter, OpenAI ([openai.com](https://openai.com)), You.com ([you.com](https://you.com)), Google Custom Search ([policies.google.com](https://policies.google.com)), and others to process your queries. Your queries and search results may be transmitted to these third-party APIs for processing. These providers may use the data according to their privacy policies.
- Third-Party Analytics: Services that help us understand how users interact with our Service.
